The Problem

Phishing attacks trick people into revealing sensitive information like passwords, banking details, or personal data. Cybercriminals create fake websites that look like real ones to fool users. These attacks cause financial loss and identity theft.

Our Solution

PhishLens is a smart detection tool that checks suspicious URLs in real time using AI. It scans for phishing patterns, warns the user instantly, and allows community reporting of malicious websites. With future upgrades, it will also compare the website's visual design to the real brand to detect advanced attacks.
